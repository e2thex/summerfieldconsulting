The Coaching Habit Course

Build Best-in-Class Coaching Culture

An 8-week course designed to develop strong coaching habits among your organization's leaders, combining book club discussions with practical training and accountability.

Perfect for mentors, managers, tech leads, and anyone committed to improving their coaching skills.

Course Components

Weekly Workshops

One-hour synchronous sessions with your cohort for discussion and practice.

Dedicated Channel

Cohort-specific Slack channel for ongoing discussion and support.

Daily Practice

Apply learnings in your daily work with guided accountability.

8-Week Schedule

Course Schedule and Topics
Week Topic Master Class
Week 1 Introduction Ask One Question at a Time
Week 2 "What's on your mind?" Cut the Intro and Ask the Question
Week 3 "And what else?" Get Comfortable with Silence
Week 4 "What's the real challenge here for you?" Acknowledge the Answer
Week 5 "What do you want?" Use "What" Instead of "Why"
Week 6 "How can I help?" Listen with Curiosity
Week 7 "What was most useful for you?" Get Specific with Follow-up
Week 8 Integration & Practice Building Lasting Habits
